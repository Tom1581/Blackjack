Privacy Policy for Hi-Lo Blackjack Trainer

Last updated: May 25, 2026

Hi-Lo Blackjack Trainer is a mobile game and blackjack practice app. This Privacy Policy explains what information is handled when you use the app.

Information We Collect

We do not require users to create an account, sign in, or provide personal information such as a name, email address, phone number, or payment details.

The app may store gameplay information locally on your device, such as bankroll, game progress, settings, and practice statistics. This information is used only to provide the game experience and is not sent to our own servers.

Advertising

Hi-Lo Blackjack Trainer uses Google AdMob to show ads. Google and its partners may collect and use information such as device information, advertising identifiers, approximate location, ad interactions, diagnostics, and other data to provide, limit, measure, and improve ads.

You can learn more about how Google uses information from apps that use its services here: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

You can learn more about Google advertising here: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

Children's Privacy

Hi-Lo Blackjack Trainer is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

Real-Money Gambling

Hi-Lo Blackjack Trainer is a practice and entertainment game. It does not offer real-money gambling, real-money prizes, betting with real currency, or the ability to cash out virtual chips.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We may share information only as needed for services used in the app, such as Google AdMob, or if required by law.

Data Security

We use reasonable measures to protect the app and the information handled by it. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

Your Choices

You may reset or limit your device advertising ID using your device settings. You may also uninstall the app at any time to stop all app activity on your device.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at the developer support email listed on the Hi-Lo Blackjack Trainer Google Play store page.
